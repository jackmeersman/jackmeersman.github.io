About Me

I'm a cybersecurity professional specializing in digital forensics and incident response (DFIR), with significant experience in both reactive incident handling and proactive security improvement projects.

Currently serving as a Manager at Alvarez & Marsal's Global Cyber Risk and Incident Response Services, I lead security incident investigations while also helping clients strengthen their security posture through various initiatives including cyber incident response planning, tabletop exercises, and purple team assessments.

With a background spanning multiple industries, including healthcare, financial services, entertainment, and manufacturing, I bring a diverse perspective to solving complex security challenges. My technical expertise is complemented by a strong understanding of security compliance frameworks including PCI DSS, ISO 27001, HITRUST, NIST CSF, and FedRAMP.
